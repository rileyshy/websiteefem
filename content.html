About FiveM

FiveM is a multiplayer modification framework for GTA V, allowing you to play on customized multiplayer servers.

Features

  • Custom game modes and scripts
  • Roleplay communities
  • Racing servers
  • Freeroam servers
  • Much more!

How to Get Started

To get started with FiveM, download the client from their official website and join your favorite server!

Community

FiveM boasts a vibrant community of gamers and developers who create unique experiences every day.
